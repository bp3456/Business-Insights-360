## Dashboard Views & Insights

### 1. Finance View
Engineered a dynamic P&L statement to monitor critical financial KPIs.

Key Metrics: Net Sales, Gross Margin %, and Net Profit %.

Purpose: Provides a deep dive into the company's financial health across different time frames and regions.

### 2. Sales & Marketing View
Utilized scatter plots and trend lines to analyze customer performance versus product profitability.

Insights: Identified high-potential customer segments and underperforming products.

Impact: Focused on identifying growth opportunities to drive a projected 10% revenue increase.

### 3. Supply Chain View
A specialized view to track Forecast Accuracy and Net Error.

Optimization: Categorizes products into "Excess Inventory" or "Out of Stock" risk profiles.

Goal: Helps the warehouse team minimize operational overhead.

### 4. Executive View
A high-level, consolidated dashboard designed for C-suite leadership.

Visibility: Provides instant insights into Revenue by Division and Market Share trends.

Performance: Optimized using DAX Studio to ensure the fastest load times for executive review.

## Technical Highlights
Data Volume: Modeled over 1 million records from MySQL and Excel.

Optimization: Saved 25% storage space and improved performance by 10% using DAX Studio.

Modeling: Built on a robust Star Schema architecture for scalability.
